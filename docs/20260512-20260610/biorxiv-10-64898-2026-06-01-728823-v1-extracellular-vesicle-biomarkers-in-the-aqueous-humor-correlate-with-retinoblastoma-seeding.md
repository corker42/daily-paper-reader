## 摘要
目的：探究视网膜母细胞瘤（RB）房水（AH）来源的细胞外囊泡（EV）表面标志物，特别是CD133，与RB临床特征的相关性。

方法：使用基于磁珠的流式细胞术对从RB眼诊断时（n=17）或初次摘除时（n=5）收集的房水样本（n=22）进行分析。量化四跨膜蛋白（CD9、CD63、CD81）和CD133的中位荧光强度（MFI），并与国际眼内RB分期（IIRC）、种植位置、种植形态及摘除手术进行相关性分析。

结果：在22个样本中有16个（73%）检测到CD133+ EVs。虽然CD133水平随IIRC分期进展呈非显著上升趋势，但玻璃体种植眼的CD133表达显著高于仅视网膜下种植眼（P=0.010）。Cuzick趋势分析显示，CD133% MFI升高与复杂种植显著相关（P<0.001）。相反，CD63呈现出显著的反向趋势，% MFI降低与复杂种植相关（P<0.001）。CD133高表达眼（MFI>10,000，n=9）的摘除率增加（P=0.007），且以球形/云状种植模式为主（P=0.001）。CD133高表达眼呈现协调的CD63/CD81共表达模式（r=0.80），而在CD133低表达组中缺失。

结论：CD133水平和百分比的升高与玻璃体受累及复杂种植形态相关，提示其可作为疾病负荷的微创指标。CD63与种植严重程度的负相关，以及CD133高表达眼中CD63/CD81重塑，提示存在一种指示侵袭性疾病的EV图谱。这些发现支持CD133+ EVs与晚期RB种植的关联，并需进一步前瞻性验证。

## Abstract
PurposeTo investigate the correlation of retinoblastoma (RB) aqueous humor (AH)-derived extracellular vesicle (EV) surface markers, particularly CD133, with clinical features of RB.

MethodsAH samples (n=22) collected at diagnosis (n=17) or primary enucleation (n=5) from RB eyes were profiled using magnetic bead-based flow cytometry. Median fluorescence intensities (MFI) of tetraspanins (CD9, CD63, CD81) and CD133 were quantified and correlated with International Intraocular RB Classification (IIRC) stage, seeding location, seeding morphology, and enucleation.

ResultsCD133+ EVs were detected in 16 of 22 (73%) samples. While CD133 levels showed a non-significant increasing trend with advanced IIRC stages, CD133 expression was significantly elevated in eyes with vitreous seeding vs. subretinal only (P = 0.010). Cuzicks trend analysis revealed a significant association between rising CD133 % MFI and complex seeding (P < 0.001). Conversely, CD63 displayed a significant inverse trend, with decreasing % MFI associated with complex seeding (P < 0.001). CD133high eyes (MFI>10,000, n=9) exhibited increased enucleation (P = 0.007) and predominantly sphere/cloud seeding patterns (P = 0.001). CD133high eyes demonstrated a coordinated CD63/CD81 co-expression pattern (r = 0.80), absent in the CD133low group.

ConclusionsElevated CD133 level and percentage were associated with vitreous involvement and complex seeding morphology, suggesting potential as a minimally invasive indicator of disease burden. The inverse relationship between CD63 and seeding severity, combined with CD63/CD81 remodeling in CD133high eyes, suggests an EV profile indicative of aggressive disease. These findings support the association of CD133+ EVs with advanced RB seeding and warrant further prospective validation.
